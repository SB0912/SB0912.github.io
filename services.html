Our Services

Data-driven expert witness services for high-stakes wage-and-hour litigation.

Litigation outcomes often hinge on data that is incomplete, inconsistent, or difficult to interpret. We specialize in reconstructing that data into clear, defensible evidence. We work with attorneys on wage-and-hour and FLSA disputes to design case data strategy, analyze complex records, and produce damages models that hold up under scrutiny. Our focus is not just analysis, it’s building evidence that stands in deposition and trial.

What We Do

Our work includes:

  • Reconstruction of work-hour and compensation timelines
  • Development of defensible damages models
  • Data validation and methodology documentation for opposing counsel
  • Trial-ready visualizations and expert reports

Why It Matters

Weak data creates risk. Strong data creates leverage. Our analyses have directly contributed to materially improved outcomes in seven-figure disputes, including:

  • A 75% reduction in claimed damages for the defense
  • A $700,000 settlement supported by validated overtime analysis

Our Approach

We don’t just analyze data, we build the foundation it stands on. Every engagement is structured around:

  • Methodological transparency
  • Reproducibility of results
  • Alignment with legal strategy
  • Clarity for non-technical audiences

The result is analysis that is both technically sound and legally effective.
